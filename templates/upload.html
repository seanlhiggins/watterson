Overview

Watterson is a basic CSV upload tool that allows you to bulk manage users and groups outside of your Looker interface.

Rather than spending time manually adding Users, then single clicking to add them to Groups and then adding User Attributes, Watterson takes your pre-prepared CSV of Users and gives you options after uploading regarding how to process them.

You can choose to add all users directly to Groups, create corresponding Group/User Attribute pairings, apply default values to all users
